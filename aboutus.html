About Us

We want to bring better banking solutions to the Columbia community.

Mission Statement
To operate a student-run, not-for-profit financial cooperative dedicated to furthering the financial well-being of the Columbia University community.

What is a credit union?
A credit union is a community-oriented, not-for profit financial institution that offers all of the services that a traditional bank does, but which specifically serves the members of a specific community. For the Lion Credit Union, this community is comprised of all students, faculty, staff, and alumni of Columbia University, as well as members of their immediate family. This means that we are dedicated to tailoring everything from our rates and products to our perks and customer service just for you.

What benefits does a credit union offer?

Better Rates: We provide better savings and interest rates than commercial banks do, while providing the same services at of the same level of quality and security -- meaning that up to $250,000 of your money is federally insured.

Better Service: We are here to address your wants and needs. Our team is composed of some of the brightest and most passionate people at Columbia who are here to serve you, and furthermore we are backed by a well-established partner credit union.

Better Community: One of our main priorities is to invest back into the community. We plan to offer better services and rates, free financial literacy classes, and a sense of community for our members.
