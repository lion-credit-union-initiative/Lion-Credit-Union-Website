Benefits


There are many benefits of banking with the Lion Credit Union. Some of them include:





Better Rates
Better Service
Better Community
We have better savings and interest rates than traditional banks do, while providing the same services at of the same level of quality and security - meaning that up to $250,000 of your money is federally insured.
We are here to address your wants and needs. Our team is composed of some of the brightest and most passionate people at Columbia who are here to serve you, and furthermore we are backed by a well-established partner credit union.
One of our main priorities is to create a sense of community for our members. We plan to build a sense of community through member-only events, free financial literacy workshops, and a visible on-campus presence.
