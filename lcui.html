Welcome to the Lion Credit Union

Who We Are

LCU provides a comprehensive range of financial products to meet the needs of the Columbia community, including the students, staff, alumni, and organizations of Columbia University.

Why We're Here

To ameliorate the quality of life of the Columbia community by providing reliable products that are affordable, accessible, and tailored to the needs of our members.

USALLIANCE Financial

In February of 2016 we established a partnership with USALLIANCE Financial, a $1+ billion not-for-profit financial institution serving over 90,000 members worldwide that constitutes the partner credit union of several renowned corporations such as American Express, PepsiCo, and IBM. Through our partnership with USALLIANCE Financial we can provide the Columbia community wıth a full suite of banking products at industry-competitive rates and tailor these services to fit the needs of our members. With a seat in the institution’s board of directors, supervisory committee, and Asset-Liability Committee (ALCO), we can ensure our members that their interests and needs will be represented within USALLIANCE.
