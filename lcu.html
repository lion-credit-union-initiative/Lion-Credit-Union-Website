Who Are We?

The LION Credit Union is a not-for-profit financial institution that serves Columbia students, staff, faculty, alumni, and their immediate families with a full suite of banking products. We're a division of USALLIANCE Financial, a $1+ billion not-for-profit institution serving over 90,000 members and organizations such as American Express, PepsiCo, and IBM. Through our partnership, we’ve committed to tailoring our offerings to community needs and to providing the Columbia community with superior products at above-industry rates. All LCU accounts are insured by the National Credit Union Share Insurance Fund (a federal agency) up to $250,000 per individual, so you can rest easy knowing your hard-earned savings are protected.
