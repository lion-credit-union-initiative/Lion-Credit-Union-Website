About LCU

What is LCU?
LION Credit Union (LCU) is a non-profit cooperative banking initiative originally established by a group of Columbia students who wanted to bring a better banking solution to the Columbia community. We operate as a division of USALLIANCE Financial, a federally chartered credit union. You can read more about our story here!
Who is USALLIANCE and what is your relationship with USALLIANCE?
USALLIANCE Financial is a $1 billion not-for-profit, federal credit union serving around 100,000 members including employees of PepsiCo, American Express, and IBM. They have been nationally recognized for their ability to rapidly integrate new technologies to meet members' evolving needs. USALLIANCE is LCU's partner institution, through which we offer our suite of financial products. To learn more about USALLIANCE, check out their website here.
Who runs LCU?
LCU is run by a team of Columbia students and an advisory group of senior level USALLIANCE executives!
Who is eligible to join LCU?
LCU membership is open to all Columbia students, staff, faculty, alumni, and their immediate families!
Are international students eligible to join LCU?
Absolutely!
Are you federally insured? Is my money safe?
Yup! Through our partnership with USALLIANCE, funds are insured up to $250,000 per member by the National Credit Union Administration, a U.S. federal agency.
What is your routing number?
Our routing number is 221981063.
When are financial literacy events coming?
LCU and USALLIANCE share a deep commitment to financial education. We'll be unveiling our financial wellness program in the very near future, so stay tuned! :)
What financial products do you offer?
Currently, we offer checking accounts, savings accounts, and credit builder loans - a special product USALLIANCE developed to help members establish or rebuild their credit histories. We are working hard on expanding our line of financial offerings, and we expect to make some exciting announcements in the near future!
Will you offer student loans, credit cards, or other financial products?
Those are certainly on our radar along with many other products! Check our website and watch our member emails for updates as new products become available.
How can I get in touch with LCU if I have any questions?
If you have any questions specifically related to your account, contact the USALLIANCE Member Service Center. Representatives are available to help you Monday through Friday 7:30am - 7:00pm, and Saturday 9:00am - 1:00pm. You can also go to usalliance.org and chat with a Member Services Representative online.

If you have any questions about LCU in general, shoot us an email at lioncreditunion@gmail.com!
How can I get involved with LCU?
If you want to join the LCU team, apply during the fall or winter recruiting cycles. You can send a letter of interest out to us at lioncreditunion@gmail.com!
