LCUI FAQ

About LCUI

What is LCUI?
LCUI is a non-profit financial institution that serves Columbia students, staff, faculty, alumni, and their immediate family. We are a division of USAlliance Financial. For more information, see check out this page.
Who runs LCUI?
LCUI is a division of USAlliance Financial, a $1+ billion not-for-profit institution serving over 90,000 members and organizations such as American Express, PepsiCo, and IBM. LCUI is run by a team of Columbia students and USAlliance employees.
Who is eligible to join LCUI?
All Columbia students, staff, faculty, alumni, and their immediate family are eligible to join LCUI.
Are you federally insured? Is my money safe?
Yes, your money is safe with us! All accounts with LCUI are insured (through USALLIANCE) by the National Credit Union Share Insurance Fund (a federal agency) up to $250,000 per individual.

Checkings Accounts

How much interest do I earn on checking accounts?
Checkings accounts do not earn interest.
What debit card do I get?
Checkings accounts will get VISA debit cards.
How or when will I get my debit card?
Your debit card will arrive in your physical mailbox in x weeks at the address you entered when you signed up for your account.
How many ATMs can I access?
You can access any of USALLIANCE’s 6000 ATMs in New York city and 25000 nationwide. Additionally, we’ll reimburse you for ATM fees at non-USALLIANCE ATMs up to $15 per month. Find an ATM here!
Where can I find an ATM?
You can find an ATM here! Or, you can use the nearest non-USALLIANCE ATM and get reimbursed for ATM fees up to $15 per month.
How do I access my checkings accounts?
