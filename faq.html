LCU FAQ

About LCU

What is LCU?
LCU is a non-profit financial institution that serves Columbia students, staff, faculty, alumni, and their immediate family. We are a division of USAlliance Financial. For more information, see check out this page.
Who is USALLIANCE and what is your relationship with USALLIANCE?
USALLIANCE Financial is a credit union and a $1+ billion not-for-profit financial institution serving over 90,000 members and organizations such as American Express, PepsiCo, and IBM. USALLIANCE is also LCU's partner institution; we offer our financial products through USALLIANCE. For more information on credit unions, take a look here.
Who runs LCU?
LCU is a division of USAlliance Financial, a $1+ billion not-for-profit institution serving over 90,000 members and organizations such as American Express, PepsiCo, and IBM. LCU is run by a team of Columbia students and USAlliance employees.
Who is eligible to join LCU?
All Columbia students, staff, faculty, alumni, and their immediate family are eligible to join LCU. To sign up, just go here or click the Join Us button at the top of this page!
Are international students eligible to join LCU?
Yes, of course international students are eligible to join! To sign up, just go here or click the Join Us button at the top of this page!
Are you federally insured? Is my money safe?
Yes, your money is safe with us! All accounts with LCU are insured (through USALLIANCE) by the National Credit Union Share Insurance Fund (a federal agency) up to $250,000 per individual.

What financial products do you offer?
As of fall 2017, we offer checking accounts, savings accounts, and credit builder loans. We are working hard on expanding our line of financial offerings, so stay tuned for exciting developments in the coming months!
Will you offer student loans, credit cards, or other financial products?
We certainly hope to! We're working hard on expanding our line of financial offerings, so stay tuned for exciting developments in the coming months!

How can I get involved with LCU?

Checking Accounts

How do I open a checking account?
To sign up, just go here or click the Join Us button at the top of this page!
How much interest do I earn on checking accounts?
Checking accounts do not earn interest.
What debit card do I get?
Checking accounts will get VISA debit cards.
How or when will I get my debit card?
Your debit card will arrive in your physical mailbox in x weeks at the address you entered when you signed up for your account.
How many ATMs can I access?
You can access any of USALLIANCE’s 6000 ATMs in New York city and 25000 nationwide. Additionally, we’ll reimburse you for ATM fees at non-USALLIANCE ATMs up to $15 per month. Find an ATM here!
Where can I find an ATM?
You can find an ATM here! Or, you can use the nearest non-USALLIANCE ATM and get reimbursed for ATM fees up to $15 per month.
