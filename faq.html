LCU FAQ

About LCU

What is LCU?

LION Credit Union (LCU) is a non-profit cooperative banking initiative established by a group of Columbia students who wanted to bring a better banking solution to the Columbia community. We operate as a division of USALLIANCE Financial, a federally chartered credit union. You can read more about our story here!
Who is USALLIANCE and what is your relationship with USALLIANCE?

USALLIANCE Financial is a $1 billion not-for-profit, federal credit union serving around 100,000 members including employees of PepsiCo, American Express, and IBM. They have been nationally recognized for their ability to rapidly integrate new technologies to meet members' evolving needs. USALLIANCE is LCU's partner institution, through which we offer our suite of financial products. To learn more about USALLIANCE, check out their website here.
Who runs LCU?

LCU is run by a team of Columbia students and an advisory group of senior-level USALLIANCE executives.
Who is eligible to join LCU?

LCU membership is open to all Columbia students, staff, faculty, alumni, and their immediate families.
Are international students eligible to join LCU?

Absolutely!
Are you federally insured? Is my money safe?

Yes! Through our partnership with USALLIANCE, funds are insured up to $250,000 per member by the National Credit Union Administration (NCUA), a U.S. federal agency.
What is your routing number?

Our routing number is 221981063.
When are financial literacy events coming?

LCU and USALLIANCE share a deep commitment to financial education. Stay tuned for the financial wellness program that we'll be unveiling in the near future!
What financial products do you offer?

Currently, we offer checking accounts, savings accounts, and credit builder loans - a product USALLIANCE developed to help members establish or rebuild their credit histories. We are working hard on expanding our line of financial offerings, and we expect to make some exciting announcements in the near future!
Will you offer student loans, credit cards, or other financial products?

Those are certainly on our radar along with many other products! Watch our website and email updates for information as new products become available.
How can I get in touch with LCU if I have any questions?

If you have any questions specifically related to your account, contact the USALLIANCE Member Service Center. Representatives are available to help you Monday through Friday 7:30am - 7:00pm, and Saturday 9:00am - 1:00pm. You can also go to usalliance.org to chat with a Member Services Representative online.

If you have any questions about LCU in general, shoot us an email at lcu@usalliance.org.
How can I get involved with LCU?

If you want to join the LCU team, apply during the fall or winter recruiting cycles. You can send a letter of interest out to us at lcu@usalliance.org.

Checking Accounts

How do I open a checking account?

To sign up, click here or the Join Us button at the top.
How much interest do I earn on checking accounts?

Although your checking account does not earn interest, you'll have the benefit of no monthly maintenance fees, no minimum balance requirements and a free monthly credit score.
What debit card do I get?

You'll be getting a Visa® debit card in the mail.
How or when will I get my debit card?

If you ordered a card at the time of account opening, your debit card will be mailed to the address we have on record within 5 to 7 business days.
How many ATMs can I access?

You'll have access to over 6,000 surcharge-free ATMs in the New York city area and over 25,000 surcharge-free ATMs across the country. Additionally, we’ll reimburse you for ATM fees at non-USALLIANCE ATMs up to $15 per month. Find an ATM here!
Where can I find a surcharge-free ATM?

You can find an ATM here to find the closest ATM to you! Don’t worry if you can’t find one – your checking account comes with a monthly rebate of up to $15 for non-USALLIANCE ATM fees.
How do I access my checking accounts?

You can access your account 24/7 through online banking here. Or, you can download the USALLIANCE mobile app for iOS for Android.

Savings Accounts

How do I open a savings account?

To sign up, click here or the Join Us button at the top.
How much interest do I earn on savings accounts?

Currently, you'll earn 3.05% APY on the first $500 and 0.02% APY on the remaining balance. APY is Annual Percentage Yield.
How do I access my savings accounts?

You can access your account 24/7 through online banking here. Or, you can download the USALLIANCE mobile app for iOS for Android.
