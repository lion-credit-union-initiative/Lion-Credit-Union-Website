Work With Us!

Joining LCU is a great opportunity for people with different backgrounds and interests. We have roles in finance, operations, marketing, relations, and technology. Joining LCU provides students with real-world experience and the ability to partake in a venture that directly helps the community. As a member of the organization you will be able to partake in numerous unique experiences in fields that interest you while building a credit union at Columbia. Below contains more detailed information about the various teams within LCU and you can access our application below the descriptions. We look forward to hearing from you!

The Operations Department is responsible for executing the immediate day-to-day logistics of the credit union, as well as envisioning and implementing plans to ensure the success of our next steps. Operations manages the staff of the credit union, and is in charge of researching and generating business strategy relevant to the union’s entrepreneurial needs.

The Finance Department is crucial in ensuring the success of the credit union’s business as well as maintaining the financial stability of our assets. Finance predicts and keeps track of the credit union’s performance through generating projections, financial models and indexes, as well as our engagement with our members in terms of account status and activity.

The Marketing Department creates and promotes the brand of Lion Credit Union to the Columbia University community. Through researching and identifying the needs of various communities on campus, we market the credit union’s products with tailor-made approaches in physical and digital marketing to maximize our presence in the University.

The Technology Department is in charge in perfecting the Credit Union’s mobile and online banking experience, a particularly integral aspect of our business in the rapidly digitizing landscape of modern-day banking. It ensures that our members have access to an effective, user-friendly and secure technological interface to utilize our products and services.

The Relations Department initiates and maintains Lion Credit Union’s business and advisory relationships within as well as outside the Columbia University community. It is responsible for contacting faculty, administrators, student groups as well as the media to promote the Credit Union’s mission and inform them of the latest developments in our business.
